Why do you need a survey?

Reasons you may need a survey

    Before a title to land is conveyed
    For the preparation of a subdivision
    Before land is improved by constructing drives, fences, walls and buildings.
    Before land is partitioned by will or by court order.
    When a question arises as to the location of a boundary line between you and your neighbor.
